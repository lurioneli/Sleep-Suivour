How It Works

Simple steps to transform your health habits into victories.

1

Start a Fast

Choose your fasting goal (16:8 is great for beginners) and tap Start. The timer begins counting immediately.

2

Log Powerups

During your fast, tap powerups as you do them: drinking water, having black coffee, going for a walk, or exercising. Each one boosts your damage.

3

Watch Monsters Take Damage

Head to the Slayer tab to see your damage in real-time. Your fasting hours attack the Visceral Fat Beast while your sleep hours strike the Insulin Dragon.

4

Track Sleep & Eating

Log your sleep sessions and rate how you feel. Track your meal quality to boost your eating multiplier for even more dragon damage.

5

Build Streaks & Climb Leaderboards

Consecutive days of fasting and good sleep build streak bonuses. Sign in to compete on global leaderboards and track your all-time stats.
